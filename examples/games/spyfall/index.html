    How to play

    A secret location is dealt to everyone — plus a role there. One player is the SPY and sees neither.
    1. Take turns asking each other pointed questions out loud.
    2. Non-spies: expose the spy without naming the location.
    3. The spy: blend in & deduce where you are.
    4. Hit Reveal anytime (or when the timer runs out).

    Deals to everyone currently in the room. Need at least 3 players.
